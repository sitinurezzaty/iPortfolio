My Favourite Books


Call Upon Him By Mizi Wahid

In this book, it discuss 21 practical steps on how we can nurture our relationship with Allah through our Du'a. It also teaches us how to strengthen our trust when we are required to stay in faith throughout the long wait after the Du'a has been made.

You are Loved By Mizi Wahid

In this book, it told us the concept of love and the impact of it in our lives. This book is not just emotionally inspiring but it also spiritually empowering. When I had a bad day, this book help me to gain back my confidence , when I think that no one who loves me.
